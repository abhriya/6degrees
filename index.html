Want someone's number? Ask your friends.

6degrees is your private, self-updating phonebook. Search for contacts on your friends' phones, and get introductions through your friends.

Get it on Google Play Watch A Demo

Automatic contact updates.

Update your details on all your friends' phones remotely by simply updating your own phone. Similarly, your friends can update their details on your phone by simply updating their own phone. If all your friends join 6degrees, your contact list becomes self-updating.

Search and discover.

Search for people and find results from friends' phones. To see their contact details, request your friend.

Search for nearby places - cafes, shops, plumbers - and find them on friends' phones or on their own 6degrees page.

Total privacy.

You decide who can see your details and who can't. Hide your contacts from appearing on your friends' search results.

Back up and restore.

6degrees backs up your contacts with unlimited storage. Restore them to any device and never lose your contacts again.
