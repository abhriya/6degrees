App Xpert

'Right app at the right time'

Introducing 'App Xpert', an app recommendation engine that finds most relevant apps for you

Below are the steps to install App Xpert widget

  1. Download 'App Xpert' from the link.
  2. Go to your Device Settings -> Security and Enable "Allow installation of application from both trusted and unknown sources" (This will enable you to install apps from sources other than Play Store)
  3. Install the 'App Xpert'
  4. Longpress homescreen and select 'Widgets' from the list
  5. Select and drag 'App Xpert' widget to a screen on your device.
